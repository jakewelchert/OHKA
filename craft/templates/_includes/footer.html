Why We

We went on a visit today and the client said her son has beein in the ICY 3 times (near death) because of his asthma and has had 4 asthma attacks in the past 3 months. We weren't initially going to be able to help his family as the home needed work far beyond our budget but the client's partner decided he would be willing to do a portion of the work. Because of his contribution, Omaha Healthy Kids Alliance feels confident we can make the home healthy enough to have a great impact on their child's asthma. Definately the highlight of our week.

- Halie Smith, Health Communication Specialist
